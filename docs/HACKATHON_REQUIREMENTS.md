# Vibe2Ship requirements — source of truth for implementation

## Selected problem
Problem Statement 2: **Community Hero — Hyperlocal Problem Solver**.

The product must help citizens identify, report, validate, track, and resolve community issues using collaboration, data, and intelligent automation. AI value must be visible in reporting, verification, tracking, and resolution rather than being a decorative chatbot.

## Product thesis
CivicLens is a transparent civic-resolution coordinator that:
1. consolidates duplicate citizen evidence into one canonical case while preserving every contribution;
2. uses a real tool-using Gemini workflow to create a sourced, human-reviewed resolution plan;
3. compares before/after evidence and recommends closure or reopening without pretending to be a government portal.

## Truth boundary
CivicLens does **not** claim to submit complaints to, integrate with, or receive acknowledgement from government systems unless a real integration is implemented and demonstrated. Generated complaints, RTI text, authority suggestions, and escalation documents are drafts for human review.
